Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll be downgraded to the Free tier. Your workspaces are preserved, but premium features are disabled.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Is Python really running in the browser?

Yes! We use Pyodide, which is CPython 3.11 compiled to WebAssembly. It's real Python, not a simulation.

What are the Node.js limitations?

We use QuickJS, a lightweight JS engine. It supports ES2020 but not npm packages. Built-in modules like path, crypto, and os are available.

How does license validation work?

Licenses are validated via Lemon Squeezy's API. Works offline with cached validation.
